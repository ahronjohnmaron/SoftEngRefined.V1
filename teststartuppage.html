Getting Started

1. Account Setup

To begin using CENTRANOUNCEIT DEPTHUB, administrators need to:

  • Navigate to the admin login page
  • Enter your registered email address
  • Receive and enter the OTP (One-Time Password) sent to your email
  • Complete the verification process

2. Dashboard Overview

Once logged in, you'll have access to:

  • Announcement Manager: Create, edit, and delete announcements
  • User Management: Manage member accounts and permissions
  • Analytics: View announcement reach and engagement metrics
  • Settings: Configure system preferences and notifications

Creating Announcements

Standard Announcement

  1. Click the "New Announcement" button
  2. Fill in the title, description, and category
  3. Set priority level (Normal, Important, Urgent)
  4. Select target audience or departments
  5. Add attachments if needed
  6. Preview and publish

OCR Form Auto-Fill

Our OCR feature allows you to quickly create announcements from physical documents:

  1. Click "Create from Image"
  2. Upload or capture a photo of the document
  3. Wait for OCR processing (usually 2-5 seconds)
  4. Review and edit the extracted information
  5. Publish the announcement

Search & Filtering

Find announcements quickly using our powerful search features:

  • Keyword Search: Search by title, content, or tags
  • Date Range: Filter by specific time periods
  • Category Filter: View announcements by department or type
  • Priority Level: Sort by urgency
  • Status: Filter active, archived, or scheduled announcements

Security Features

OTP Authentication

All admin access is protected by One-Time Password authentication:

  • OTP codes are valid for 10 minutes
  • Codes are sent to your registered email
  • Maximum 3 login attempts before temporary lockout
  • Sessions expire after 24 hours of inactivity

Data Protection

  • All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Regular automated backups
  • Role-based access control
  • Audit logs for all administrative actions

Mobile Access

Access CENTRANOUNCEIT DEPTHUB on any device:

  • Fully responsive design for phones and tablets
  • Touch-optimized interface
  • Offline viewing of cached announcements
  • Push notifications for urgent updates

Troubleshooting

Common Issues

Not receiving OTP codes?

  • Check your spam/junk folder
  • Verify your email address is correct
  • Wait 2-3 minutes for delivery
  • Request a new code if expired

Unable to upload images for OCR?

  • Ensure image is less than 5MB
  • Supported formats: JPG, PNG, PDF
  • Use clear, well-lit photos
  • Avoid blurry or skewed images

Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Q: What is CENTRANOUNCEIT DEPTHUB?

A: CENTRANOUNCEIT DEPTHUB is a centralized announcement management system designed to streamline communication across departments. It allows administrators to create, manage, and distribute announcements efficiently while providing members with easy access to important updates.

Q: Who can use this platform?

A: The platform is designed for educational institutions, organizations, and businesses that need to manage departmental communications. Administrators create and manage content, while members can view and search announcements.

Q: Is my data secure?

A: Yes, we use industry-standard encryption, OTP authentication, and regular security audits to protect your data. All communications are encrypted, and we never share your information with third parties.

Account & Access

Q: How do I log in as an administrator?

A: Click "Admin Login" in the navigation menu, enter your registered email, and complete the OTP verification sent to your email. The OTP is valid for 10 minutes.

Q: I forgot my password. What should I do?

A: Our system uses OTP-based authentication, so you don't need to remember a password. Simply enter your registered email, and you'll receive a new OTP code to access your account.

Q: Can I have multiple administrators?

A: Yes, you can add multiple administrator accounts with different permission levels. Contact support to set up additional admin accounts.

Q: How long does my session last?

A: Admin sessions remain active for 24 hours of inactivity. After that, you'll need to log in again for security purposes.

Managing Announcements

Q: How do I create a new announcement?

A: From your admin dashboard, click "New Announcement," fill in the required fields (title, description, category), set the priority level, select your target audience, and click "Publish."

Q: Can I schedule announcements for later?

A: Yes, when creating an announcement, you can select a future date and time for automatic publication.

Q: How does the OCR feature work?

A: Upload a clear photo of a physical document, and our OCR technology will extract text automatically. You can then review, edit, and publish the content as an announcement.

Q: Can I edit or delete published announcements?

A: Yes, administrators can edit or delete any announcement at any time. However, editing history is logged for transparency.

Search & Organization

Q: How do I find old announcements?

A: Use the search bar to enter keywords, or apply filters by date range, category, priority level, or department.

Q: Can I archive announcements?

A: Yes, you can archive announcements to remove them from the main feed while keeping them accessible through the archive section.

Q: How are announcements organized?

A: Announcements are categorized by department, priority level, and date. You can customize categories to match your organization's structure.

Technical Support

Q: What browsers are supported?

A: CENTRANOUNCEIT DEPTHUB works best on the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. We recommend keeping your browser updated.

Q: Can I use the platform on mobile devices?

A: Absolutely! Our platform is fully responsive and works seamlessly on smartphones and tablets.

Q: What file types can I upload?

A: You can upload JPG, PNG, PDF for OCR, and various document formats as attachments (PDF, DOC, DOCX, XLS, XLSX).

Q: Is there a file size limit?

A: Images for OCR should be under 5MB. Document attachments can be up to 10MB each.
